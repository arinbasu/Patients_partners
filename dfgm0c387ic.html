Methods

We conducted a search of Medline, CINAHL, and Ovid to capture maximum number of scholarly publications in the past five years to capture current state of literature review and we used the terms "P4 medicine" or "P4 health" or "participatory health" or "participatory medicine" as words appearing in either the title of the  research publications or as title words to further capture as many publications as possible. Only English language articles and articles published in peer-reviewed publications were considered for inclusion in the review.
